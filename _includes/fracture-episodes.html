## What is *Failure to Fracture*? [*Failure to Fracture*](/learn/fracture) is a video series I've put together about all the things that have stood in the way of my successfully playing the song *Fracture* by [King Crimson](/discover/king-crimson) despite years and years of study, practice, writing, and making videos. ## Episode List - [Episode 0: Introduction](/learn/fracture) - [Episode 1: Stress and Tension](/learn/fracture/stress) - [Episode 2: Picking Picks](/learn/fracture/picks)
